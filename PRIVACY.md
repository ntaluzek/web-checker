# Privacy Policy for Web Checker

**Last Updated: November 24, 2025**

## Your Privacy

**The extension developer does not collect, access, or receive any of your data.** All information you create while using Web Checker stays entirely on your device and within your browser.

## Data Storage

Web Checker stores data locally in your browser to provide its functionality. This includes your URL lists, preferences, and settings.

This data is stored using Chrome's storage API, which means:
- **Data stays in your browser** and your Google Chrome profile
- **Data syncs across devices** if you're signed into Chrome with sync enabled
- **No data is transmitted to the extension developer** or any third-party servers
- **The developer has no access to your data** - it remains entirely within your Chrome browser and Google account

## Data Sharing

**Web Checker does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any of your data.**

The extension developer:
- Has **no backend servers**
- Receives **no data** from your use of the extension
- Has **no access** to any information you create or store
- Cannot see what websites you visit or what you do with the extension

All data remains exclusively within your browser and Google's Chrome Sync service (if enabled).

## Third-Party Services

Web Checker does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the extension or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated "Last Updated" date at the top.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
